The wettest hour

by Jeff Frankl and Casey Thomas, Jul. 3rd, 2013

This past June has been the wettest June on record in Philadelphia.

When does the most rain fall?

The Philadelphia Water Department maintains a network of 24 rain gauges that are spread through out the City. We've looked at all the data collected starting in 1990 through last month, and tallied the total amount of rain that has fallen in each hour of each month.

Source: Philadelphia Water Department

Note: During times of snowfall, the perciptation amounts recorded by the rain gauges do not represent the amount of snowfall. Once the snow melts, the rain gauges record the amount of melted snow in the gauge.
